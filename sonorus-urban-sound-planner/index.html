SONORUS: Urban Sound Planner

Since the summer of 2014 I am working at the Swiss Federal Laboratories for Materials Science and Technology, more commonly known as Empa. I am employed as an Early Stage Researcher within the SONORUS project.

SONORUS is an Initial Training Network and it's goal is to educate future Urban Sound Planners.
